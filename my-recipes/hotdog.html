Hotdog

Have you ever wondered how to make your own hotdog? From now on you will never need to buy overpriced street food again.

A list of ingredients:

  • A hotdog bun
  • Sausage
  • Mustard

Preparation steps:

  1. Slice hotdog bun lengthwise into two connecting halves
  2. Put sausage in a sliced hotdog bun
  3. Pour some mustard on top of sausage
  4. Enjoy!
