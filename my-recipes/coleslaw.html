Coleslaw might not be the healthiest type of salad nor the most appealing but it sure never hurts to try.

A list of ingredients:

  • A head of cabbage
  • Some carrots
  • Mayonnaise

Preparation steps:

  1. Cut cabbage into small pieces
  2. Do the same with the carrots and mix chopped vegetables together
  3. Pour mayonnaise on top
  4. Enjoy!
