Refreshing apple pie is a time-tested American dessert.

A list of ingredients:

  • Dough (you can buy it in the store)
  • Apple jam

Preparation steps:

  1. Unfreeze the dough
  2. Take part of the dough and shape it into a bowl
  3. Pour the apple jam into the dough bowl
  4. Shape the rest of the dough into several thin strips
  5. Criss-cross the dough strips on the top of the jam
  6. Bake the pie in the oven for an appropriate amount of time
  7. Enjoy!
